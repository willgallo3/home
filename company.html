Company page about us and our development team

Dynamic Dev-Team

SAFE-FS is being developed by a dynamic group of programmers, working around the clock in multiple timezones. We are regularly making hires on a per-deliverable basis, and the success of our crowdsale has determined much of the extent of the roles that will be required for the application.

SAFE-FS & SafeAppStore

SAFE-FS and the SafeAppStore are two separate projects, each with its own set of funds and team of developers. This ensures that the two projects can be developed independently and concurrently.
